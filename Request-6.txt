6.  Generate a report which contains the top 5 customers who received an 
average high  pre_invoice_discount_pct  for the  fiscal  year 2021  and in the 
Indian  market. 

select c.customer_code,c.customer,
round(avg(f.pre_invoice_discount_pct)*100,2) as customer_avg_percentage
from dim_customer c
join fact_pre_invoice_deductions f 
on f.customer_code = c.customer_code
where f.fiscal_year = "2021" and c.market = "india"
group by c.customer_code
order by customer_avg_percentage desc
limit 5;
